Mary Elizabeth Truss (born 26 July 1975) is a British politician who has served as prime minister of the United Kingdom since 6 September 2022. After 45 days in office, she announced on 20 October her intent to resign as prime minister and leader of the Conservative Party as soon as a succesor is chosen [1][2][failed verification] She previously held Cabinet positions under prime ministers David Cameron, Theresa May and Boris Johnson, including serving as foreign secrtretary from 2021 to 2022. She has been Member of Parilament (MP) for South West Norfolk since 2010. Ideologically, she is considered a Thatcherite and right-librtarian.
